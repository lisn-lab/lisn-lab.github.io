Mental Health Innovation Hub

Reshaping Youth Mental Health Research Through Self-Understanding and Regulation

⛰️ Our Mission

We are launching a pioneering initiative to transform how mental health research is conducted with and for young people. Our focus is on understanding and supporting experiences of inner speech and voice hearing through technology, led by the very people it aims to serve. We believe that meaningful mental health solutions can only emerge when researchers and young people work together as equal partners in discovery.

💫 Vision

To create a supportive, innovative community where young people, researchers, and developers collaborate to understand how self-talk and inner speech affect mental wellbeing, ultimately developing tools that make a real difference in people’s lives.

🎯 Immediate Goals (2025)

  • Build a diverse community of student researchers and advisors
  • Conduct regular focus groups to understand youth perspectives on mental health and technology
  • Develop and test initial prototypes for monitoring self-talk
  • Create a framework for youth-led mental health research

📈 Long-term Impact

  • Transform how mental health research is conducted
  • Develop innovative tools for supporting mental wellbeing
  • Create opportunities for young people to shape mental health solutions
  • Build a sustainable model for participatory research
